Flowers and Love

Roses symbolize love, royalty, beauty, sensuality, secrecy, and mysticism.
They are also associated with achievement and perfection.
Roses are prized around the world, among the most beloved and popular flowers.
Their fragrance is instantly recognized, but complex and distinct between varieties.



Just like red roses, the tulip is a symbol of passion and intense love.
It is the ideal flower for Valentine's day.
It is a popular flower in european countries such as France.



Dahlias symbolize elegance, creativity, positivity, and growth.
It also represents inner strength, likely due to the plant's ability to tolerate harsh conditions.
Other general meanings associated with the dahlia is gracefulness, standing out in a crowd, and embracing positive changes.
